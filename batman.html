BATMAN

Bio

Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, is a fictional superhero created by writer Bill Finger and artist Bob Kane for DC Comics. He first appeared in May 1939 in Detective Comics no. 27. Bruce Wayne is a billionaire industrialist and playboy by day, but transforms into Batman, the protector of Gotham City, by night.

Bruce Wayne's origin story involves the murder of his parents, Thomas and Martha Wayne, when he was a child, which he witnessed. This tragic event inspired him to dedicate his life to fighting crime and avenging his parents' deaths. Inspired by the bats that lived in the Wayne family property, he adopted the persona of Batman to instill fear in criminals.

is known for his lack of superpowers; instead, he relies on his genius-level intellect, peak physical condition, and advanced technology to combat crime. He has a vast knowledge of various fields including science, forensics, and medicine, and is often referred to as the "World's Greatest Detective".

Allies include Robin, Batgirl, Commissioner Jim Gordon, and Alfred Pennyworth, his loyal butler and guardian. He is also a member of various teams such as the Justice League and the Outsiders.
