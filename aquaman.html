AQUAMAN

Bio

Aquaman, also known as Arthur Curry, is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. Created by writer Mort Weisinger and artist Paul Norris, Aquaman first appeared in More Fun Comics #73 in 1941. He is the half-human, half-Atlantean ruler of Atlantis and possesses the ability to communicate with marine life, swim at incredible speeds, and withstand the pressures of the ocean depths.

Arthur Curry was born to a lighthouse keeper, Thomas Curry, and Atlanna, the queen of Atlantis. After discovering his heritage, he embraced his role as Aquaman and protector of the seas.
