WONDER WOMEN

Bio

Wonder Woman, also known as Princess Diana of Themyscira, is a superhero created by William Moulton Marston and first appeared in All Star Comics #8 in October 1941.

She is an Amazon princess and the daughter of Queen Hippolyta and Zeus, though in her initial origin, she was depicted as a clay baby brought to life by the patron goddess Aphrodite. Raised on the hidden island of Themyscira, also known as Paradise Island, Diana is an Amazon and her people's gift to humanity.
